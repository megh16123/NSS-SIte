About Us

National Service Scheme

NSS Cell of Maharaja Surajmal Institute started its venture in January, 2020 with selfless nature, enthusiasm and zeal to give back to the society and bring about a positive change with over 200 enthusiastic volunteers. Our aim is to identify the needs and problems of the community and involve them in the process of problem-solving as we go by the motto 'Not me, but you'. Our vision is to spread awareness and love while ringing in a propitious change in our society.
