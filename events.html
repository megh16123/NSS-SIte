Events Organized

Fitness Fuesday

On a warm sunny day of April 19th, 2022, an offline fitness session comprising of Zumba, Aerobics, Yoga and Meditation was organized to increase the stamina of the students by a very renowned International Yoga Instructor Dr. Kush Panchal. Sir has been a trainer to International actors like Emma Watson and Indian actors like Kangana Ranaut, Shahid Kapoor, etc. The session was divided into two phases. The first session took place on the lawn outside the auditorium. In that session, Sir made all the students do warm up exercises which consisted of light stretching, pranayam, bhangra and a few steps of aerobics too. In the second phase, which was conducted in the auditorium, Sir shared his journey of becoming a fitness coach and International Yoga Instructor. The event was a great success in making students understand that workout can be a fun activity and it also very well taught the students the importance of being fit.

Empowering the Power: Break the Bias

Project Veerangana organized this interactive and inspiring event on the occasion of International Women’s Day, 8th March 2022, with the theme for this year being ‘Break the Bias.’ Its main aim was not only to remind people that women are strong, but rather to make women themselves recognize their strength. The event was interactive and lighthearted but also conveyed its message strongly. The ecent started off with an enthralling speech by the project heads and core members of Project Veerangana, and then moved on to a series of fun activities. The volunteers truly did a spedid job engaging the audience and recieved a lot of appreciation for this event.

Secret Santa

This event was planned keeping in mind the festivities and the essence of Christmas and was executed to make this Christmas one of the best our elders would have celebrated. Since Santa is believed to bring gifts for children on Christmas eve, so the volunteers of Project Vriddhi thought we should do the same for our elders who themselves deserve to be loved and pampered as children when they grow older. This event was divided into 2 parts. Day one was all about collecting the gifts and goodies to be donated to the elderly and making them cards to be gifted to make them feel special. About 90 volunteers came to college to donate and make cards for the elderly, the number of gifts collected was greater than 200. The next day was all about visiting the old age home and distributing gifts and spreading happiness among the elders. Team Vriddhi decided to visit the “Silver Lining Old Age Home” in Dwarka. Before distributing gifts and cards, the volunteers interacted with the elderly, played games with them, sang their favourite songs, danced with them, and listened to their games. After the completion of the event, the smiling faces of the elderly and the happiness which we could sense radiating from them was worth all our efforts.

Aatmarakshan

On 9th November, 2021, NSS MSI organized an offline event “Aatmarakshan” with live self-defense lessons and demonstrations. The chief guest for the afternoon was Major Love Chauhan. The event kicked off with a lively interaction between the audience and Major Chauhan followed by a variety of demonstrations where volunteers joined him on stage for a "kickboxing" session. He skillfully and steadily displayed his moves while the volunteers carefully took note of his movements and did their best to learn. The event was a great success and extremely interactive, all the attendees took away a lot of valuable lessons from the program. We were honored to have Major Chauhan with us, his lessons will always be treasured by us.

Mini Olympics

Regular physical activity is proven to help prevent and manage noncommunicable diseases such as heart disease, stroke, diabetes and several cancers. It also helps prevent hypertension, maintain healthy body weight and can improve mental health, quality of life and well-being. To pay tribute to the Tokyo 2020 Olympics and promote a healthy lifestyle among students, NSS MSI organized the NSS Mini Olympics and Fit India Freedom Run 2021 in August 2021. Many competitive and fun games were organized for the students, such as Sprint, Tug of War, Badminton etc. After all three games were concluded, all participants gathered for the prize distribution. Our Director, Mrs. Rachita Rana, gave a speech of appreciation for the NSS Volunteers and the entire Administrative Council, for the day was a massive success! All attendees then rose for the National anthem before the prize distribution.

Nature Walk

On the 24th of September, Project Avani under NSS MSI, hosted their first offline event 'Nature Walk' at the Northern Ridge in Delhi. The group was led by Dr A.K Singh, a professor at Delhi University. The group enjoyed a relaxing morning walk in the cool embrace of mother nature. They learned about the various characteristics of the flora and the surrounding wildlife along with some interesting trivia. The cool, calm and relaxing air of the forest, paired with the excellent insight by the leader was welcome by all, especially after being stuck at home due to the pandemic for such a long time. The event ended with fond memories, brand new information and a new sense of belonging with mother nature for everyone.

Rewired, not Retired.

Project Vriddhi, under NSS MSI organized a webinar “Rewired not Retired”, on the occasion of World Elder Abuse Awareness Day. The main aim of this webinar was to play our part in spreading awareness about the relevant topic of elder abuse, a critical social problem. The event, held on 15 June, 2021, had to be conducted in online mode due to Covid restrictions. It commenced with an interview of world renowned and one of the oldest sharp shooter Prakashi Tomar, who is the inspiration behind the bollywood movie "Saand Ki Aankh" starring Taapsee Pannu and Bhumi Pednekar. She spoke about various topics like women empowerment and ageism. Another interview was held with very famous Instagram influencer Shanti Devi Sethia, who, at the age of 76, practices Yoga as well as makes educational as well as entertaining Instagram reels. The event was a massive success and both the speakers truly inspired us all.

NCC Awareness Campaign

This event was held to raise awareness among the students about the National Cadet Corps (NCC). The NCC aims at developing character, comradeship, discipline, a secular outlook, the spirit of adventure and ideals of selfless service amongst young citizens. The seminar covered various important subjects: about NCC, what it takes, the financial aspect, and much more. The speaker helped us get a glimpse of all the multi-folds of the functioning of NCC from a close lens, so as to give us clarity as to what we can expect, if we opt to be a part of it. The college auditorium was filled with 100% capacity, over 500 students, and there were many students who were waiting outside to be a part of the event. The event was very interactive as we were given the opportunity to ask any doubt that we had in our minds regarding NCC and its functioning.

Talk-It-Out: A Sex Ed Webinar

Sex education is vital for teens to help them take wise decisions about their sexual partners & understand all about consent. This also reduces premature or undesired pregnancy rates & transfer of STIs. On the 20th of January, 2022, Project Yuva under NSS MSI invited Dr Sidhaant Nangiat and organized an educative sex education webinar for the students. The webinar covered important topics like personal hygiene, importance of protection, STD’s and much more. Dr Sidhaant assisted us in our aim of breaking the taboo that surrounds sex, porn, sexuality and such topics by freely discussing them and answering students’ genuine doubts and questions that surround it.
